PROCESS

3 Gallons of Milk (Not UltraPasteurized)
- If using raw milk decrease culture by
25-40%
1/2 tsp (2.5ml) Calcium Chloride (for pasteurized milk)
3 ml (a bit over 1/2 tsp) Single Strength Liquid Rennet
1 Packet C101 Mesophilic Culture
Salt

Large Colander
Good Thermometer
Cheese Wax or Cloth for aging
Butter Muslin
Cheese Press or Weights
Spoon or Ladle to Stir Curds
Knife to Cut Curds
Medium Hard Cheese Mold
Brush for Waxing

Heat & Acidify Milk
Coagulate with Rennet
Cut The Curd & Release Whey
Cook Curds
Drain & Cheddar The Curds
Mill & Salt The Curds
Form & Press The Curds
Prepare for Aging
